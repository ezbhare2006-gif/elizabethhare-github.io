My Passions & Interests

Travel

Ive enjoyed traveling for as long as I can remember. I had early exposure as my parents are from different sides of the country so seeing family required a lot of traveling. I spent many summers in my moms home state of Wyoming as a kid, and once youve done the drive across the plains of eastern Colorado enough times anything seems possible.

My parents instilled the "its not about the destination its about the journey" philosophy in me and I appreciate the expieriences that has lead to. This hobby has lead to many netwoekign oppertunities that have made the journey more than worth while. Beign able to meet and connect with people from all over the world has been a huge part of my life and I am so grateful for the connections I have made through travel.

This hobby has given me a deep appreciation for how diverse and beautiful the world is. I have been able to see so many different cultures, landscapes, and ways of life that have broadened my perspective and made me more open-minded. I am excited to continue exploring new places and meeting new people in the future.

Sports

Growing up, my dad was an athletic director at the University of Kansas, and I would spend many afternoons after school or away games with the team expieriencing the behind the scenes of college athletic. It gave me a deep appreciation for the magnitude and passion that goes into athletics for everyone invovled, and because of that its alwasy been a special space to me.

I have loved having the oppertunity to be a part of the athletic community at UMD as a production assisstant for the athletic department. I have been able to work with the video production team and contribute to the game day experience for fans and student athletes. I have also been able to connect with many people in the athletic department and learn about the various roles and careers in sports.

This has always been an avenue I have seen potential in pursuing as a career, and I am excited to continue exploring the sports industry and finding ways to combine my passion for sports with my skills. It would be incredible to work in sports media, where I can help teams and organizations gain more exposure.
